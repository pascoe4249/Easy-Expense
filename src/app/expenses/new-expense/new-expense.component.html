Guide

  1. Give your expense a title. This could be the name of the course you had to attend, or the event you were part of.
    e.g Safety Course

  2. Add a brief description of what it involved. Some extra justification or information.
    e.g Travel to London

  3. Enter the date the trip, event or course started. This is to identify different claims to the same location.

  4. Click the check, in the top right, to confirm. You will be able to add individual receipts later by clicking on the claim, from the Expenses Log page.

  5. Clicking the cross, in the top left corner, will cancel creation and take you back.

About

Welcome to the Easy Expense App. Created by Richard Pascoe as part of the JHUB Coding Scheme, Module 2 Assignment.

How it Works

You will open on the 'Expenses Log' page. This is where you can view each expense you have created.


Each expense item shows you the title or name of the trip, course, event etc. that you attended. It keeps track of the total you are claiming for and shows a brief description, along with the date.


Clicking the plus icon, in the top right, will start a new expense claim. You will need to fill out a few details and then confirm, upon which you will be able to see that new claim under any others.


Clicking on the expense item, will take you into that expense detail page. This is where you can view all of the receipts you will be claiming, under that expense. You will also see the total of all receipts and a button to delete the expesne.


Each receipt will have it's own item where it will display where the receipt came from, what type of claim it is, the amount and the issue date. It will also display a photo of that receipt.


You can add a new receipt by clicking the plus icon, in the top right corner. Fill out all the information, exaclty as it appears on the receipt and then take a picture to prove it. Once submitted, this receipt will be present on the expense detail page. clicking the cross will cancel the creation and take you back.


By clicking on the receipt item, you can edit the information, in case you made a mistake. Click submit to confirm any changes. Or clicking away will simply return you to the expense detail. Here you also have a delete button, to remove that receipt.


Now you have all of your claims in one place, and sorted by trip. I hope this helps you organise yourself a little better. Or makes claiming back the expendature smoother.
